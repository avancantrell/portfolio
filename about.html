Designer Bio

Avan Cantrell is a preferably-faceless visual artist and designer based in Durham, NC. He has a BFA in visual art from Clemson University and is currently studying graphic and web design at Wake Technical Community College, where he has earned several certificates related to graphic design and video game development.

In his spare time he enjoys crocheting, reading LGBT rom-com books, and having movie nights with his husband and their dog. He also plays Dungeons & Dragons and fantasy RPG video games, which inspire much of his illustration and comic work.
